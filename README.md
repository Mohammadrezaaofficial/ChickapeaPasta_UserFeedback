# ChickpeaPasta_UserFeedback-Data-Science

A Company name Chickapea came to us with an objective of driving trial and brand-awareness among health-oriented consumers in urban markets. Peekage designed a study and sent a free sample of product to the users.

After running this campaign for the company, we gathered some data from the users regarding the product. You can see the context of the campaign we ran and the product that was tested by users below.

My mission is to report to company about the result of the campaign, the questions and how did the product perform according to the campaign's objective. Along with the report, you must provide an ai agent (in google colab) that can analyze and answer the questions our customer may ask. 

## 1. Campaign Context  

### Campaign Summary  
This campaign promotes **Chickapea Spirals** pasta across 27 U.S. states. The product—made from chickpeas and yellow peas—delivers **24 g protein** and **8 g fiber** per 100 g serving, offering a nutritious yet familiar alternative to wheat pasta.

**Offer:** 100 %-off discount code for a free box (free shipping)  
**Objective:** Drive trial and brand awareness among health-oriented consumers in key urban markets.
